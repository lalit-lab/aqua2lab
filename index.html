Our Process

How We Work

1

Discovery & Vision

We begin with a deep conversation about your space, aesthetic preferences, lifestyle, and budget. Every masterpiece starts with understanding your vision.

2

Design & Proposal

Our designers craft a detailed layout plan with hardscape sketches, plant lists, equipment specs, and a transparent cost breakdown — presented for your approval.

3

Installation Day

Our team arrives fully equipped. We install, scape, plant, and cycle the aquarium — leaving your space clean and your aquascape ready to flourish.

4

Ongoing Care

Scheduled maintenance visits keep your ecosystem thriving. We monitor parameters, trim plants, dose nutrients, and ensure everything runs perfectly.
